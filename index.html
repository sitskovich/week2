Energy on the Move! ⚡

When an object is in motion, it has **motion energy** (or kinetic energy). When it collides with something, that energy doesn't disappear! It changes into other kinds of energy.

It's called **energy transformation**!

Motion Energy ➡️ **Sound** Energy 🔊

Motion Energy ➡️ **Electrical** Energy 🔌

Electrical Energy ➡️ **Light** Energy 💡

Wacky Waves! 🌊

Light and sound both travel as **waves**!

Waves have two main parts: **Wavelength** and **Amplitude**.

👉 **Wavelength:** The distance between the waves. Think of the distance between two waves at the beach! 🏖️

👉 **Amplitude:** The height of the wave. A taller wave is a stronger, louder sound or a brighter light! 📈

Let's find out how we can use these waves to send a message!

Wave Challenge! 🎢
